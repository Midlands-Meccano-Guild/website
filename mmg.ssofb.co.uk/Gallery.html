** TEMPORARY PROBLEM WITH THIS WEB PAGE **

Posted 13/03/2025

Links from the MMG website to the photo Gallery on the NZMeccano website are currently not working. Unfortunately this means many of the links below to photos of meetings and Meccano models cannot be accessed. This problem is under investigation, further information will be posted here and on the News page when there is any update or when it is fixed.

While the photo gallery on the NZMeccano website is down there is an alternative photo gallery available to us which Bob Thompson has set up on the Flickr website and kindly agreed to share with MMG members. This does not show the full historical library of NZ images and is being developed by Bob as a possible future solution if there are ongoing issues with the NZ photo gallery.
