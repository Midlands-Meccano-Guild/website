Legends of the Midlands Meccano Guild - Past & Present

Bert Love was one of the founder members of the MMG. Photos of legendary MMG members models are shown in this gallery














Jack Partridge, a founder member of the MMG was President from 2008 to 2012. Shown here at the French Exhibition (CAM) where he received an award in 2010. A lifelong member of MMG, he produced models in many subjects but is probably best known for his clocks. An article remembering Jack's contribution to the Meccano was published in MMG Bulletin 51. Click for pdf version Some of his models are featured here. http://www.nzmeccano.com/image-62375
