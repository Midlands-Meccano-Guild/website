EXHIBITIONS

MMG MECCANO exhibition at The British Motor Museum, home to the world’s largest collection of British Cars; it boasts nearly 300 cars in its collection which span the classic, vintage and veteran eras and is a mecca for car enthusiasts. The Heritage Motor Centre is situated in Gaydon, Warwickshire, just off junction 12 of the M40.


MMG has been invited again to exhibit at the British Motor Museum in 2025. Hope to see you there.

The Exhibition is planned for 19 & 20 July 2025.
