WELCOME TO THE MIDLANDS MECCANO GUILD

Formed in 1967 the Midlands Meccano Guild was one of the first Meccano Clubs formed in the UK aimed at an adult membership.

Members have a wide range of interests in Meccano modelling and collecting. We meet twice a year in March and October near Coventry in the Heart of England. The club welcomes enthusiasts from a wide area, including a number of overseas members.

To find out more about the Midlands Meccano Guild click here.

For more information about the club contact the Secretary (email:The Secretary).




A recent MMG meeting at Baginton Village Hall near Coventry


Members please return your meeting attendance form at least a week before the meeting.

You can complete the meeting attendance form online. If you are not a member and would like to attend please contact: (email:the Secretary).

We look forward to seeing you.

Please note there will be no provision of luncheon at the meeting. Members should bring their own food.
